## How to Use

1. **Open the Application** - Open `index.html` in your web browser
2. **Add a Task** - Type your task in the input field and click the plus button (or press Enter)
3. **Mark Complete** - Click the checkbox next to a task to mark it as complete
4. **Track Progress** - Watch the progress bar fill as you complete tasks
5. **View Statistics** - See your completion ratio (e.g., "3 / 5")

## Key Features Explained

### Progress Tracking
The app displays how many tasks you've completed out of your total tasks. The progress bar visually represents your completion percentage.

### Local Storage
All your tasks are automatically saved to your browser's local storage, so your data persists even after closing the browser.

### Empty State
When you have no tasks, a friendly illustration appears to encourage you to add new ones.
